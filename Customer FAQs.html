We are very excited to introduce our coalition Loyalty Program.

What is a coalition Loyalty Program?

This is a program which gives you the ability to redeem your Loyalty Points at various businesses that are available on our platform, which means it’s not limited to just one retailer, restaurant, ecc.

How much does it cost?

For you as a customer it’s free. As soon as you register for our service, you are automatically enrolled in our Loyalty Program. So go ahead and register and start earning points.

How do I collect points?

Simple, whenever you make a reservation through our platform, you earn Loyalty Points. You will also earn Loyalty Points if you purchase a deal through our platform.

How can I redeem them?

Loyalty Points can be redeemed from the “Loyalty Program” section on our platform. You may redeem your Loyalty Points towards anything that is available in that section, given that you have enough Loyalty Points of course.

Will points expire?

No

Can I buy points?

No. We believe that Loyalty Points should be earned every-time you use our service. So much so, that the more you use our service, we will give extra Loyalty Points whenever you achieve certain milestones.
