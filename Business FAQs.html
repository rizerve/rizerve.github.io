I have a business, what will I benefit from this Program?

When you are part of this program and you allow our customers to redeem their Loyalty Points at your business, we will give you free advertising space on our platform, that includes, website, mobile application, and also social media account - all for free

How can all of this be free?

Good question. If your business is part of our Loyalty Program, that only fee we charge is when a customer makes a redemption towards your deal and/or offer that will be available for redemption on our platform. That means, you will only pay if our customers redeem their Loyalty Points towards your deals and/or offers.

What is the fee?

The fee is that of 2% for every redemption made, and here’s how it is calculated: 1 Loyalty Point = €1 equivalent (Loyalty Points do not hold any monetary value and is just for reference purposes. - Therefore as an example: If a customer uses 50 Loyalty Points to redeem a special offer, the fee (2%) that will apply is that of - €1).

What offers do I have to make?

No one knows your business better than you, so it’s up to you to decide what you would like to offer. The only thing we ask of you, is that any offer and/or deal that will be available on our platform, can only be redeemed through our platform. You will not be allowed to offer the same deal or an equivalent deal or a better deal through any other channels and/or platforms. You may not direct customers away from our platform in order to provide them with any sort of deal.

Why do I have all of these restrictions?

Please understand that since we will be offering your business free ad space and free marketing campaigns, this comes at a cost to us. So it’s only fair that the offers that will only be available on our platform. We want this platform to be sustainable and to offer customers great service while helping your business grow.
